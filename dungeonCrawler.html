About The Project (WIP)

A procedurally generated dungeon crawler that I am working on for my major studio project. Generates random maps using cellular automata and marching squares. Created based on a tutorial by Sebastian Lague. All models were created in 3DS Max. Rock textures are from the Natural Tiling Textures pack available on the Unity asset store.
